# Go-To-Market Strategy for AI Cybersecurity Solution

## Target Audience
1. Large enterprises with security teams.
2. IT security firms and managed security service providers.

## Marketing Approach
1. Early Access for Security Professionals: Offer pilot programs for feedback.
2. Industry Webinars & Conferences: Showcase the product's AI-powered threat detection.

## Business Model
1. Subscription-Based Pricing: Monthly/annual security monitoring plans.
2. Enterprise Licensing: Custom solutions for large organizations.
